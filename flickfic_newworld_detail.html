Synopsis
A city that rewrites itself every generation. The towers are new but the foundations are old, and the old foundations remember things the towers would rather forget. Three people carry pieces of the same truth — a journalist who stopped publishing, an archivist who stopped filing, and a child who has not yet learned to stop asking. One question connects them all. No one has dared to ask it aloud. Until now.
